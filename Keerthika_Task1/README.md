# Task 1 - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data

## Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to uncover meaningful patterns, trends, and business insights. Through data cleaning, statistical analysis, and visualization techniques, the project aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of sales performance, customer behavior, and product demand.

## Objectives
Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
Analyze sales performance using descriptive statistics.
Identify trends and patterns in retail transactions.
Examine customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
Evaluate product category performance.
Create visualizations to effectively communicate insights.
Generate actionable business recommendations based on the analysis.

## Dataset

The dataset contains retail transaction information, including:

* Transaction ID
* Date
* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Product Category
* Quantity
* Price per Unit
* Total Amount

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Loading

The retail sales dataset was imported into Jupyter Notebook using the Pandas library. The dataset structure, data types, and feature information were examined to understand the available data.

### 2. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing

Data quality checks were performed to identify missing values, duplicate records, and inconsistencies. Necessary preprocessing steps were applied to ensure the dataset was accurate, complete, and suitable for analysis.

### 3. Descriptive Statistical Analysis

Basic statistical measures such as mean, median, mode, minimum, maximum, and standard deviation were calculated to summarize the dataset and understand the distribution of key variables.

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Exploratory analysis was conducted to identify patterns, trends, and relationships within the data. Customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and product performance were examined to gain meaningful business insights.

### 5. Time Series Analysis

Sales data was analyzed over time to identify trends, seasonal variations, and fluctuations in business performance. This helped in understanding sales growth and demand patterns across different periods.

### 6. Customer Analysis

Customer-related attributes such as age and gender were analyzed to understand purchasing behavior and identify key customer segments contributing to revenue.

### 7. Product Performance Analysis

Product categories were evaluated based on sales volume and revenue generation to determine the best-performing products and areas for potential business growth.

### 8. Data Visualization

Various visualization techniques, including bar charts, line plots, histograms, and heatmaps, were used to present findings effectively and facilitate better interpretation of the results.

### 9. Insight Generation and Recommendations

The results obtained from the analysis were interpreted to derive actionable insights. Business recommendations were proposed to improve sales performance, customer engagement, and decision-making processes.
