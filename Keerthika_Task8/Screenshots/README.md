# Screenshots

This folder contains screenshots of the **Play Store Intelligence Dashboard** developed for **Task 8: Unveiling the Android App Market – Analyzing Google Play Store Data**.

## Included Screenshots

### 1. Dashboard Home Page

Displays the cyberpunk-themed dashboard with KPI cards, category filters, and overall Play Store statistics.

### 2. App Distribution Analysis

Visualization showing the distribution of apps across different Google Play Store categories.

### 3. Content Rating Analysis

Pie chart illustrating the share of apps by content rating.

### 4. Rating Distribution

Histogram showing the distribution of app ratings and average rating trends.

### 5. Top Installed Apps

Bar chart displaying the most downloaded applications on the Google Play Store.

### 6. Installs vs Reviews Analysis

Scatter plot exploring the relationship between app installs, reviews, and ratings.

### 7. Monetization Dashboard

Analysis of free vs paid apps, pricing trends, and estimated revenue by category.

### 8. Sentiment Analysis Dashboard

Natural Language Processing (NLP) analysis of user reviews using TextBlob sentiment classification.

### 9. Word Cloud Visualization

Word clouds representing the most frequent terms in positive, negative, and neutral reviews.

### 10. Trends Analysis

Visualizations showing app update trends, category evolution, and Android version support.

### 11. Deep Dive Explorer

Interactive search and filtering interface for exploring individual apps and exporting filtered datasets.

## Note

All screenshots were captured from the running Streamlit application after loading the Google Play Store datasets (`apps.csv` and `user_reviews.csv`).

These screenshots demonstrate the dashboard's analytical capabilities, interactive visualizations, sentiment analysis features, and market intelligence insights.
